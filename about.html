About Me

Pioneering Smart Textile Solutions for Healthcare

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Toronto (Institute of Biomedical Engineering) and University Health Network (KITE), specializing in smart textile innovation for healthcare applications. With over 10 years of experience in textile materials engineering, product development, and advanced materials characterization, I lead cutting-edge research initiatives in biomedical textiles.

My research focuses on developing wearable solutions for healthcare, with flagship projects on monitoring and treating sleep apnea. I have successfully secured over $300,000 in competitive research funding and have established international collaborations with leading institutions.
