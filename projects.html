Featured Research Projects

Innovative solutions in smart textiles and biomedical applications

😴

Sleep Apnea Monitoring & Treatment

Flagship biomedical textile project developing wearable solutions for real-time sleep apnea monitoring and therapeutic intervention through smart textiles. This project combines advanced fabric engineering with physiological monitoring capabilities.

🔌

Knitted & Embroidered Dry Electrodes

Development of textile-based electrodes using flat-bed knitting and embroidery machines for comfortable, integrated physiological monitoring. These electrodes enable long-term, non-invasive biometric sensing directly embedded in wearable garments.

🌡️

Thermoregulating Smart Textiles

Advanced textile prototypes capable of real-time skin temperature monitoring and adaptive thermal regulation for healthcare applications. Ideal for patients requiring continuous thermal management and temperature monitoring.

💨

Respiratory Monitoring Textiles

Smart fabrics designed for continuous respiratory rate and breathing pattern monitoring with clinical-grade accuracy for healthcare settings. Successfully mentored PhD and MSc students on developing these innovative textiles.

🔬

Graphene-Based End-of-Life Sensors

Novel moisture and thermal sensors using laser-induced graphene technology for textile degradation monitoring and performance assessment. Industry partners include DuPont, Filspec, Davey Textile Solutions Inc., and Innotex.

🛡️

High-Performance Fiber Analysis

Comprehensive aging analysis of para-aramid, meta-aramid, and other high-performance fibers for firefighters' protective clothing reliability. Developed accelerated aging programs simulating real service conditions with industry partners DuPont and LION Protects.

🔐

IR Camouflage for Personal Protective Equipment

Identified and characterized potential polymeric materials for product development. Explored innovative lamination techniques for fabrication and developed functional prototypes for specialized protective applications.

🧽

Chemical & Biological Self-Detoxifying Membranes (COMFORTS)

Development of nanofibre membranes capable of chemical and biological detoxification. Comprehensive material characterization and performance testing for protective applications through various microscopy and spectroscopy techniques.

🧵

Functional Yarns via Additive Manufacturing

Developed functional yarns using additive manufacturing technology and innovative extrusion processes for NSERC-NFRF funded smartwear revolution project. Created 3D-printed flexible composites for wearable textiles and soft robotics applications.
